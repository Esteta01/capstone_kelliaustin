Work in 2030

This work reflects on the trends and signals of the existing work market with the aim to forecast the 2030 working environment. The objective is to allow individuals to prepare themselves as well as to inspire those who are responsible today for our future workplace (physical and virtual).

The workplace is more than the physical office space; it is the people we work with, the systems we use, the management style we operate under, and the space we work in. The workplace is a social institution and is constantly shifting and evolving along with society. In order to capture the various components of our future workplace, this site is divided into four parts, each representing one pillar of the 2030 workplace:

People

Who will be working?

Process

What will people be doing?

Tech

How will we partner with Technology?

Place

What is the environment in which work happens?
